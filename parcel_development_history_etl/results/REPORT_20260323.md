## 1. Purpose

This run is the first ETL run incorporating the new parcel genealogy correction system (Step 2b). The primary goals were to:

1. Apply explicit APN succession rules from `apn_genealogy_master.csv` to the stacked CSV dataframe before any join to the parcel history FC, and
2. Enable the All Parcels current service (`AllParcels/MapServer/3`) as a geometry fallback in the spatial crosswalk for APNs that have no history in the FC.

Results are compared against the March 21, 2026 baseline throughout this report.

### Step 2b — Genealogy APN Corrections (NEW)

**Input:** `apn_genealogy_master.csv` — 1,253 apply-ready rows (is_primary = 1 with change_year populated). 163 primary rows skipped due to missing change_year.

**Logic:** For each genealogy record, rows in df_csv where `APN == old_apn AND Year >= change_year` had their APN replaced with `new_apn` before `csv_lookup` was built. Substitutions were skipped for years where the new APN already existed in df_csv (21 conflict-skip events across 22 APNs).

**Result:**

| Metric | Count |
|---|---|
| Apply-ready genealogy records | 1,253 |
| Records skipped (no change_year) | 163 |
| APN substitutions applied | **65** |
| Total unit-years remapped | **2,242** |
| Conflict skips (new APN already in CSV for that year) | 21 events |

The 65 applied substitutions represent APN pairs where: (a) the old APN existed in the CSV with units, (b) the substitution year fell within the 2012–2025 scope, and (c) the new APN did not already own those year-rows in the CSV.

## 6. Recommended Next Steps

### Priority 1 — Fill Remaining 163 Unknown Change Years

163 primary genealogy rows were skipped this run because `change_year` was blank. Most of these are Washoe County `005-xxx` / `007-xxx` → `1318-xx-xxx-xxx` renumbering events that predate 2012. Bulk-entering `change_year = 2012` for all such cases would activate them on the next run.

See `GENEALOGY_REPORT_20260323.md` for the full list and instructions.

### Priority 2 — Investigate the UNKNOWN Category (111 APNs / 607 Units)

These APNs appear in the FC for some years but show 0 units for flagged years with no identifiable split pattern. This category is unchanged from the baseline. Possible causes:
- CSV data entry errors (wrong APN in CSV)
- APNs with complex non-split histories not covered by the notes
- Gaps in the parcel history FC for specific year-county combinations

Open `QA_Lost_APNs` in ArcGIS Pro, filter to `Category = UNKNOWN`, and spot-check against the parcel history FC and county assessor records.

### Priority 3 — Address 2023–2025 Deficit Spike (~-200)

The genealogy fix had minimal impact on 2023–2025 (improvement of +1 to +5 units/year). The spike remains driven by APNs newly added to the CSV for recent years that do not yet have a corresponding record in the source `Parcel_History_Attributed` FC. Steps:

1. Filter `QA_Lost_APNs` to APNs where the flagged years are exclusively 2023, 2024, or 2025.
2. Check whether those APNs exist in the AllParcels current service — if so, the source FC needs to be refreshed with recent years' parcel data.
3. If the parcels are new subdivisions or recently recorded lots, add them to `Parcel_History_Attributed` before the next run.

### Priority 4 — Validate Split Primary Assignments

65 genealogy substitutions were applied, 47 of which resolved PARCEL_SPLIT cases. For 1:N splits, the first-listed successor in the notes was designated as the primary unit recipient. Spot-check a sample of the most impactful split cases (highest `Total_Units_Moved` in `QA_Genealogy_Applied`) in ArcGIS Pro to confirm the correct successor parcel received the units.

### Priority 5 — Investigate 71% Spatial Null Rate

The persistent ~71% null rate for TAZ, Zoning, LocalPlan, and RegionalLandUse among TRPA-boundary rows has not been investigated. In ArcGIS Pro, select a sample of null rows (`WITHIN_TRPA_BNDY = 1 AND ZONING_ID IS NULL`) and overlay with the Zoning service layer to determine whether the nulls represent genuine coverage gaps or service extent limitations.

## 8. Known Limitations

- **163 genealogy records inactive:** Primary rows with no `change_year` were skipped. Filling these in the master CSV will improve the next run.
- **All Parcels service returned 0 matches:** The 10 remaining geometry-less APNs were not found in `AllParcels/MapServer/3`. These may be truly invalid APNs in the CSV.
- **1:N split unit assignment:** For split cases, all units are assigned to the first-listed successor. If a different successor is the actual unit-bearing parcel, the master CSV `is_primary` flag must be corrected manually.
- **Condo/multi-unit splits:** When one parent APN represented multiple independent units (e.g., a condo conversion creating 10+ individual APNs), the current approach assigns all units to one successor. The remaining unit-APNs show 0 until the CSV is updated to list each unit APN individually.
- **Service availability:** Spatial joins in Step 5 depend on live TRPA ArcGIS services. If a service is unavailable at run time, corresponding rows receive null values silently.
