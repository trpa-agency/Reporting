## Root Cause Fixed (This Run)

### Root Cause 5 — El Dorado Parcels Born After 2018 Not Padded

**The problem:** After Run 1 achieved −3/year across all years, the user confirmed that the three "permanently lost" APNs — `015-370-30`, `016-300-64`, `018-320-18` — are El Dorado parcels. Their 3-digit padded forms (`015-370-030`, `016-300-064`, `018-320-018`) DO exist in the FC with COUNTY=EL. They were not getting padded.

**Root cause:** `build_el_dorado_fix()` in `utils.py` only queried the FC for APNs matching the 2-digit suffix pattern (`_EL_2D`). These three parcels were **created at or after the 2018 format change** — they only ever appear in the FC in 3-digit form. Their 2-digit form is never in the FC, so `el_2d` never captured them, and they were never added to `pad_map`.

**FC presence of the padded forms:**

| CSV APN | Padded APN | FC years |
|---------|------------|----------|
| `015-370-30` | `015-370-030` | 2021–2025 |
| `016-300-64` | `016-300-064` | 2018–2025 |
| `018-320-18` | `018-320-018` | 2018–2025 |

**Fix 1 — `utils.py` `build_el_dorado_fix()`:**

Extended to also collect 3-digit El Dorado APNs (`_EL_3D`) from the FC. For any 3-digit APN whose de-padded 2-digit form is not already in `pad_map` (i.e., the parcel only exists in 3-digit form), the 2-digit → 3-digit mapping is added to `pad_map`. This is "Case 2" alongside the existing "Case 1" (parcels that span the 2018 format change).

Effect: `pad_map` grew from ~30,240 to **30,660 APNs**. The three problem APNs are now padded for Year ≥ 2018.

**Fix 2 — `s03_crosswalk.py` `_get_apn_geometry()`:**

Added "Case B" expansion (symmetric to the existing "Case A" depad expansion). For 2-digit El Dorado APNs in the S03 crosswalk list, also search for their padded 3-digit form in the FC to obtain geometry. This allows S03 to find geometry via the 3-digit form (from 2018+) and use it for centroid spatial join against pre-2018 FC layers.

Effect: All 1,021 APNs in the crosswalk list now have geometry (**0 without geometry**, vs 3 in Run 1). The centroid spatial join then matched the pre-2018 year-rows for these three APNs to the correct FC features, writing units correctly.
