Cumulative total over time

On-the-ground totals through each YEAR. Y-axis is zoomed into the data range so the year-over-year trajectory reads clearly; absolute totals are in the KPI cards above. The slider value indicates "as of" the chosen year; the chart shows the full trajectory regardless of slider position.

Data: live from Cumulative_Accounting layer 0 (Parcel Development History). The page sums Residential_Units, CommercialFloorArea_SqFt, and TouristAccommodation_Units across all parcels, grouped by YEAR and JURISDICTION. The jurisdiction filter scopes the totals; the year slider pins to the "as of" year for the KPIs (the charts always show the full 2012-2025 trajectory).
Carson County values are excluded from the jurisdiction pills (Carson does not receive Tahoe Regional Plan allocations and shows constant 4 residential units / 0 CFA / 0 TAU). Year-over-year change can be negative when banked or converted units exceed new construction in a given jurisdiction.
