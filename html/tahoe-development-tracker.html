Existing inventory (section i): live sums from Cumulative_Accounting layer 0 (Parcel Development History). Query pins to the most recent YEAR on the layer and sums Residential_Units, CommercialFloorArea_SqFt, and TouristAccommodation_Units across all parcels.
Banked development (section ii): live from Cumulative_Accounting layer 9 ("Development Right Banked By Jurisdiction"). Residential total combines MFRUU + SFRUU per the analyst's reporting framework. The per-APN layer 7 (banked by parcel) totals can differ; see data/qa_data/banked_reconciliation_summary.md.
Not reserved and not released (section iv): live from Cumulative_Accounting layer 10 (Development Right Allocation Balances) filtered to Source='Grand Total'; sums TotalBalanceRemaining per commodity (residential includes RBU per the analyst's framing).
Reserved, not constructed (section iii): live from Cumulative_Accounting layer 15 ("Development Right Reserved Not Constructed"). 3 rows (per commodity), normalized from analyst tally by scripts/convert_reserved_not_constructed.py. Eventual true derivation: layer 4 Construction_Status (2012 RP RES = 151 of the 698 total) plus a 1987 RP equivalent that isn't published today.
