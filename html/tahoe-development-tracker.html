Sources: Tahoe Regional Planning Agency, May 2026; Lake Tahoe Regional Plan, Policy DP-2.2 and Code Section 50.4.1; Development Right Pool Balance Report.
Existing inventory: live sums from Cumulative_Accounting layer 0 (Parcel Development History, the on-the-ground system of record). The query pins to the most recent YEAR on the layer (currently 2025) and sums Residential_Units, CommercialFloorArea_SqFt, and TouristAccommodation_Units across all parcels.
Regional Plan: regional_plan_allocations.json, converted from All Regional Plan Allocations Summary.xlsx by convert_regional_plan_allocations.py. "Assigned to projects" = allocations drawn to a parcel (built or in progress); "not assigned" = still available in the pool. 1987 Plan figures are hard-coded from the 2012 Regional Plan Update Analysis (not in LT Info); 2012-era figures trace to the LT Info pool balance report.
Banked: snapshot from the analyst's tally as of Mar 26, 2026 (from LT Info Banked Development Right Index). The live Cumulative_Accounting layer 7 totals can differ; see data/qa_data/banked_reconciliation_summary.md for the in-progress reconciliation.
