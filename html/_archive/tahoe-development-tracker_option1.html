Sources: Tahoe Regional Planning Agency; Lake Tahoe Regional Plan, Policy DP-2.2 and Code Section 50.4.1; Development Right Pool Balance Report.
Existing (headline number per tile): live sums from Parcel Development History. Chained query: max(YEAR) then sum Residential_Units / CommercialFloorArea_SqFt / TouristAccommodation_Units at that year.
Banked (first bar segment): live from Development Right Banked By Jurisdiction. Residential combines MFRUU + SFRUU. The per-APN Banked Development Rights totals can differ; see data/qa_data/banked_reconciliation_summary.md.
Reserved, not constructed (second bar segment): live from Development Right Reserved Not Constructed. Analyst-tally snapshot today; true derivation = Construction_Status='Not Completed' on the 2012 RP allocation grid + a 1987 RP equivalent that doesn't yet exist as row-level data.
In pools (third bar segment): live from Development Right Allocation Balances filtered to Source='Grand Total'; sums TotalBalanceRemaining per commodity (residential includes RBU per the analyst's framing).
Since-1987 capacity (tile footer): from the same Allocation Balances query, TotalAuthorized + AllocatedToPrivate per commodity.
